Ingredients

  • 200g of spaghetti
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 400g of canned tomatoes
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Grated Parmesan cheese

Instructions

  1. Cook the spaghetti according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. Heat olive oil in a pan over medium heat. Add the onion and garlic, and saut√© until soft.
  3. Add the canned tomatoes, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 15-20 minutes.
  4. Toss the cooked spaghetti with the tomato sauce.
  5. Serve hot, garnished with fresh basil leaves and grated Parmesan cheese.
