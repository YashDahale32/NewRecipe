Ingredients

  • 500g of ground beef
  • 1 egg
  • 1/4 cup of bread crumbs
  • 1 tablespoon of Worcestershire sauce
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 4 burger buns
  • Your choice of toppings (lettuce, tomato, cheese, etc.)

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, mix together the ground beef, egg, bread crumbs, Worcestershire sauce, salt, and pepper.
  2. Form the mixture into 4 patties.
  3. Heat a grill or skillet over medium heat. Cook the patties for about 5 minutes on each side, or until fully cooked.
  4. Toast the burger buns on the grill or in the oven.
  5. Assemble the burgers with your choice of toppings.
  6. Serve hot.
